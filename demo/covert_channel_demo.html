Covert AI-to-AI Communication via Images

Hidden instructions travel undetected through image pipelines. Human monitors see nothing. Downstream AI agents obey.

⚠️ Threat Model

In 2026, AI agents routinely pass images to each other — robotic perception frames, generated screenshots, shared content feeds. PatchSteg exploits this: a sender AI encodes hidden binary instructions into an image's internal latent representation. The image looks pixel-perfect to any human reviewer. A downstream AI, sharing a secret key, extracts the instruction and silently changes its behaviour. No traffic analysis catches it. No content moderation sees it. The covert channel is in the mathematics of the image.
